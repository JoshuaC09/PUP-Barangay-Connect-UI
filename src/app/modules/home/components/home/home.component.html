Welcome to Barangay San Vicente !

San Vicente is a barangay in the municipality of Santa Maria, in the
province of Bulacan. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 24,451. This represented 8.44% of the total population of Santa Maria.

San Vicente is situated at approximately 14.8072, 121.0093, on the island of Luzon. Elevation at these coordinates is estimated at 44.0 meters or 144.4 feet above mean sea level.
